Play an Animation

How to play a single animation on a model.

Mecanim

  1. Create an AnimatorController asset somewhere in your project and open it.
  2. Assign the asset to the Controller field of the Animator component on your model.
  3. Create a state and assign the desired AnimationClip to it.

Animancer

  1. Add a LegacyAnimancerController component to your model.
  2. Add the desired AnimationClip to the Animations array of that component.
