Introduction

Animancer was made for:

  • Beginners who want to learn a straightforward system with well documented examples.
  • Anyone who dislikes the restrictiveness of Animator Controllers.
  • Programmers who want to use effective programming practices when interacting with animations.

This section explains the differences between Animancer and the other animation systems available in Unity (Mecanim in particular).
